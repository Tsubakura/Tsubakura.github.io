Gladiator of Sil'dih - Accursed Visage

After dodging the lasers and dealing with another Flash of Steel, the gladiator will now use Accursed Visage. This mechanic is exactly the same as the previous mechanic, but you now have to deal with some debuffs as well that the boss applies on you.

DebuffDescriptionAdditional Notes

Silvered Fate and Gilded Fate

Remember how the lasers from the iron statues were either silver or gold? You now have to intentionally get hit by the opposite element in order to cleanse the debuff. Failure to do so will result in instant death once the timer runs out.

Remember, it is the opposite laser color that you need to get hit by, not the laser of the same color as your debuff. If you have both colors, then that means you have to get hit by one of each laser at the same time.

Identify your debuff and immediately head into a quadrant that matches the lasers that you need to cleanse your debuff. The debuffs are assigned in a way that it always allows each player to have their own quadrant, so try avoid clumping together with another player in the same quadrant.

Identify your debuffs and immediately seek out a quadrant that matches the lasers that you need to cleanse your own debuffs.

You only have one chance to cleanse your debuffs, so make sure to pick the correct quadrant.
The boss will now cast Wrath of Ruin, which summons orbs at the walls that will fire line AoEs in a grid pattern. Preposition to avoid the orbs while making sure that you will still get hit by the lasers.
Once the first set of the line AoEs resolves, you should then immediately move diagonal of your safe tile to dodge the other line AoE. Nothing Beside Remains should resolve and assuming everyone remained in their own quadrant, there should be no accidents.
