Damage down penalties​

DebuffDescription
Damage Down

Taking any damage that results from failing a mechanic will inflict a damage down. The observed damage penalty is at least -90%, effectively making dying and reviving more optimal than just letting the timer run out in most cases.
Mark of Mortality

Newly introduced in this ultimate, this debuff is applied whenever a stack mechanic was not soaked by the minimum required amount of players. E.g. a stack meant for 4-players is only soaked by 3-players, resulting all three players getting a Mark of Mortality.

Functionally, this debuff is the same as a damage down and should be avoided at all cost.
