This is the second phase of P12S and it serves as a checkpoint. For phase one, head back to [part 1](/savage/p12s_p1) of this fight.

The strategies used for each datacenter are as follows:
- Aether uses [pastebin](https://pastebin.com/gc93tBFY).
  - Aether uses the [updated version of Papan strategy](https://ff14.toolboxgaming.space/?id=286982839316861&preview=1) for Caloric Theory 1.
- Mana follows [Nukemaru](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5c3-Rinh6lA).
  - Check whether the PF mentions KX or XX, which refers to the Palladian Ray spreads during The Classical Concepts.
  - Mana parties do not line up for Pangenesis by default, but they are aware that it is a thing.
- Elemental follows [Nukemaru](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5c3-Rinh6lA), but with a few changes in the strategies:
  - Check whether the PF mentions KX or XX, which refers to the Palladian Ray spreads during The Classical Concepts.
  - Elemental uses the [original version of Papan strategy](https://ff14.toolboxgaming.space/?id=845983862306861&preview=1) for Caloric Theory 1.
  - Elemental parties always line up for Pangenesis by default.

## Major mechanics
- Gaiaochos 1
- The Classical Concepts 1
- Caloric Theory 1
- Pangenesis
- The Classical Concepts 2
- Caloric Theory 2
- Gaiaochos 2
