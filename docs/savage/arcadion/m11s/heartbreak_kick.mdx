The final mechanic, <strong>Heartbreak Kick</strong>, is a party stack tower that hits multiple times. This mechanic is repeated three times, serving as the soft enrage before the Tyrant wipes your party off the face of Eorzea with <Enrage>Heartbreaker</Enrage>. 

The standard method of dealing with this mechanic is 822:
 - The first tower is resolved having all eight players stack inside the tower.
 - The second tower is resolved by having the two tanks taking turns invulning, in the order of MT > ST.
   - You can swap places once the invuln timer is at 5s.
 - The third and final tower is resolved by having both tanks kitchen sink their mitigations.
   - The third tower appearing also coincides with 10:30, which is your 3rd pot window if your party is holding your burst.
