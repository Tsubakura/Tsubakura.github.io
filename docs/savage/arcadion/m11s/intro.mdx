## Setup

There are two waymarks going around which are used to resolve [Majestic Meteor](/savage/m11s/majestic_meteor). Elemental has used the middle-safe waymark during early weeks, while Mana (game8) uses the corner-safe waymark instead. Avoid using the Elemental week 1 waymarks, as they have caused wipes in JP parties due to the confusion in the different safe spots.
