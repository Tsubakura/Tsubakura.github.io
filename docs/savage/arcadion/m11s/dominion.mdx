The Tyrant will teleport back to the middle and start casting <Raidwide>Dance of Domination Trophy</Raidwide>. This attack plays out exactly the same way as the normal mode version, but as an added extra, the boss will target either all supports or all DPS with a 2-man stack marker.

<KittenAsset path='m11s/dominion_intro.webm' wrapper='div' />

Don't forget to mitigate this cast, as this attack also functions as a **physical** raidwide.

## Dance of Domination

Identify immediately where the open area, because that is the new relative north. Upon identifying the new north, you want to spread out in pairs on the designated safe spots so that each pair can safely take their 2-man stack. 

<KittenAsset path='m11s/dominion_game8_setup.png' wrapper='div' />

That's all there is to this mechanic, there's no other tricks.

<KittenAsset path='m11s/dominion_example.webm' wrapper='div' />

Some PFs might prefer to assign static positions to the melees, instead of having them rotate together with the new north. In this scenario, MTD1 will go to either waymark A or D, while STD2 will go to either B or C, whichever is safe.

Double check the PF description though to confirm whether this is the case, otherwise you can safely assume that they are doing the Game8 full relative north.


## Raw Steel Trophy

After resolving the dominion, the boss will use <strong>Raw Steel Trophy</strong> again to draw either an axe or scythe. This will always be the opposite of whichever you got on the first <strong>Raw Steel Trophy</strong>.
