Begin at the top left corner at the ring of the boss, as quite some things will happen in this mechanic.

## Act 1

The debuffs are fairly straightforward, as one role group has to deal with a spread while the other role group has to deal with a stack. However, the boss will try to make this mechanic a bit harder by throwing some attacks at your party, in the hope that in the confusion your party members will PvP each other with the cone shooting debuff.

Overally, an easy first mechanic which is very likely hindered by some PvP action from your party members. Killing your party members by accident is very easy here and this is usually the first wall when it comes to getting a clean run in order to meet the strict DPS check in case of an undergeared party.
