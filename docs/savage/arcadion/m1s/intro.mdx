Game8 follows [Inumaru](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=S1SBXo9tLfo), but PF is still split between inumaru and [nukemaru](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Txqnv1oBHG4):
- At the time of this writing, PF is still split between doing boss-relative and true north for the Nine Lives mechanics.
- Inumaru resolves both protean baits cardinally, while nukemaru does DPS intercardinal.
- Raining Cats is partially cheesed by using invulnerability from the tanks, with inumaru and nukemaru both having their own strategy for cheesing it.
- Anything else is the same:
  - The one-tile strategy is used for Mouser.
  - Splintering Nails is fanned out TDH from left to right.
