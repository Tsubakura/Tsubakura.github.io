Game8 follows nukemaru, who has split his videos into [part 1](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=gl4BNKLr3rk) and [part 2](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=vFh4YWxUVrE).
- There is no checkpoint in this fight, wiping anywhere during this fight means that you will start all the way back from the beginning.
